Undo a commit or range of commits.

This reverse-merges one or more revision into the current working copy. 

  > svn undo 45
  > svn undo 45:50

This will merge in 45:44 and 50:45 respectively. The source to merge from is the current working copy URL by default, but you may specify your own: 

  > svn undo 5034 ^/my-project/trunk

[Validate]
